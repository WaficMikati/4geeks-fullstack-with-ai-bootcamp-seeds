💻 **Declaration:** NO — this block introduces the conceptual principles of background processing (delegation, idempotency, decoupling, observability, durability). The student's practice is understanding and applying these principles, not writing code that executes and produces output. The hands-on implementation (cronjobs, queues) is covered in later blocks.
